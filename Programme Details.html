Programme Details

Day 1: Monday, 23 June | 3:30 PM – 6:00 PM
10 shortlisted teams from each track (AI and Advanced Manufacturing) will pitch in a closed-door session to separate judging panels, with 5 finalists per track advancing to the next stage.
Day 2: Tuesday, 24 June | 3:00 PM – 7:00 PM
5 finalists per track will pitch to an audience of around 100 attendees, including leading VCs, PEs, corporate investors, and industry experts. Finalists are also welcome to showcase and demo their offerings at display booths. The event will conclude with an award presentation and a networking dinner.
Incentives
- 10 finalists will be invited to Suzhou for the finals on 8–9 July, with 2 nights of complimentary twin-sharing hotel stay and USD 1,000 travel reimbursement per team.
- Teams that set up a company in Suzhou within 12 months will receive RMB 500K–2M in cash support, plus additional incentives from the Suzhou government.
